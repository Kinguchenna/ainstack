Enhance your song output using SSML (Speech Synthesis Markup Language):

Insert a pause

Mary had a little lamb <break time="1s"/> whose fleece was white as snow.

Emphasize lyrics

I told you I <emphasis level="strong">really like</emphasis> that place.

Adjust song tempo

Slow down for a mellow vibe: <prosody rate="slow">This is sung slowly.</prosody>
Or speed up for energy: <prosody rate="fast">Now we sing faster.</prosody>

Change pitch for musical effect

High pitch for a catchy tune: <prosody pitch="+20%">This sounds higher.</prosody>
Low pitch for a deeper tone: <prosody pitch="-20%">This sounds lower.</prosody>

Adjust volume for dynamics

<prosody volume="x-soft">This is very soft.</prosody>
<prosody volume="loud">This is louder than normal.</prosody>

Voice styles (if supported)

<mstts:express-as style="cheerful">I'm feeling great today!</mstts:express-as>

Role-play multiple singers

[singer:John] How are you doing?
[singer:Emily] I'm fine. Thank you!
Note: Replace names with your actual lyrics. Use SSML per singer if needed.
